ABOUT

_____________ THE PROJECT

This academic project was developed in the scope of the curricular unit Multimedia Design Workshop and the goal was to create an exhibition, featuring the work of a female designer, that took place on Colégio das Artes in Coimbra. Our group planned LAGEN, an exhibit dedicated to Daniela Haufe that showcases her differentiated work in an unique way, creating an experience for every visitor.
