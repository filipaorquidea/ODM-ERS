LAGEN

_____________ WHAT IS LAGEN

The exhibition delves into the innovative work of Daniela Haufe, a designer renowned for her intricate use of layers in her poster designs. Haufe's artistic process involves the meticulous layering of elements to create depth, complexity, and a unique visual narrative. This technique not only defines her style but also invites viewers to explore the subtleties and nuances embedded within each piece.
Titled "Lagen," the exhibition takes its name from the German word for "layers," encapsulating the essence of Haufe's artistic exploration. Each poster on display serves as a testament to her mastery of layering, where colors, textures, and forms converge to reveal multifaceted stories. Through this exhibition, visitors are encouraged to immerse themselves in Haufe's layered world, appreciating the interplay of visual elements that characterizes her work.
"Lagen" is more than just an exhibition; it is an invitation to witness the transformative power of layers in graphic design. It highlights how Haufe's approach transcends traditional boundaries, offering new perspectives and deeper
engagement with visual art.

Join us to experience the rich, layered landscapes of Daniela Haufe's
imagination and creativity.
